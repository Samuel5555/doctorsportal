Our Mission

Empowering every individual to Stay Healthy 24/7

We are on the road to bridging the healthcare gap in Nigeria by delivering top-tier medical care through a connected, digital platform.

Your health's best friend

Doctors Portal focuses on health awareness and personal health monitoring by top health professionals and doctors and enables:
  • Live video consultations and management for patients with diagnosed diseases.
  • Free access to curated health tips and information by healthcare professionals
  • Reduction of quality-healthcare costs and logistical challenges
  • Patient-Centered Digital Clinical Practice and Quick Emergency Response
  • General Prevention of Diseases

Fear no more, Japa no more!

Doctors Portal provide opportunity for doctors on the platform to earn in dollars and enjoy their profession more. And for the people, Doctors portal aim to eliminate the costs of quality healthcare including:
  • The cost of Discomfort
  • The cost of Distance
  • The cost of Inconvenience
  • The cost of Ignorance
  • The cost of Inefficiency and many more.

We are connecting Doctors to Patients People

Doctors Portal is the first of its kind and the leading Telemedicine platform that doesn't just only connect doctors to patients but also to the people and general public. Most times the environment a person lives in or the lifestyle of a person usually are great predictors of the kind of health conditions that may befall that person. So, Doctors Portal draw doctors closer to people in such a way that they can pick up these signals of environment, lifestyle and other risk factors and ameliorate them thus enhancing prevention, early diagnosis, prompt treatment and reducing complications and drug misuse.
